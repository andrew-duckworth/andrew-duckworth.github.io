Emotional Intelligence

What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional Intelligence is the ability to understand your own and other people's feelings. It is also about being self aware and understanding how your feelings might affect you.

How is EQ different to IQ?

EQ is measured by someone's emotional intelligence, this could be how well they are able to manage their own emotions, their self-awareness and their empathy. IQ is used to measure someone's cognitive abilities such as problem solving. EQ is also something that can be developed through practice.
