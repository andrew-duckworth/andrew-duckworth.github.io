What is the difference between Margin, Border, and Padding?

In this blog entry I will be talking about the differences between margin, border and padding.

What is the Margin?

The margin serves at the outermost layer between elements, it is used to create space around elements like an image.

For example, if you had two images on top of each other and you wanted to seperate the images you would change the margins.

I will be using pictures of my dog Cloud as examples :)

What is the Border?

The border is the line that wraps around an element. Unlike the margin the border creates a separation that you can see.

Borders can also be changed depending on what you want, for exmaple the radius, colour, width and style can all be edited.

What is Padding?

Padding is the space between the content and border. This is useful when you do not want the content to be touching the borders.
